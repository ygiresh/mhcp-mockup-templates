A Group of Gay, Lesbian, Bi-Sexual, Transgender, Asexual and Questioning Adults from

McHenry County, Illinois.

Our group meets weekly on every thursday for open discussion, freindship and support.
Our weekly topics range from many issues we face living OUT in the suburbs. We also have many social events throughout the year !
Come join us OUT here in McHenry County, IL !!!

To become a member of our group, please see the following simple steps.

STEP-1
To be part of our group you need to have a meetup.com account, if you already have an account in meetup.com, Login to your account and proceed to Step-2.

If you don't have an account with meetup.com, Go to https://www.meetup.com and signup for an account, its FREE !

STEP-2
Go to the group's meetup page https://www.meetup.com/McHenry-County-Pride/#Overview and click on "Request to Join" button and answer few simple questions under profile questions and click "Next"
STEP-3

The Group admin will contact you regarding the details via meetup.com or meetup app and You MUST come to atleast two meetup events to become a member of our group.

STEP-4

If you complete the above three simple steps, Walla!!! You'll be our new member in the group. Hope to see you soon.
